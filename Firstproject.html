New York Fashion Week 2018

Fall/Winter Collections Unveil at Fashion Week

Fashion Week began in New York City as celebrities, designers, and models all came together to preview the highly acclaimed styles for the upcoming season. As fashion week wraps up in the big apple, we went from seeing Jeremy Scott's sci-fi inspired collection to Tory Burch's Jackie O inspired collection, fall/winter of 2018 will bring together a range of trends. Let's see what London, Paris, and Milan have yet to show us. Stayed tuned and be ready to see what hits the stores.
